ABOUT US

Web3 events provide a unique opportunity for people to learn about and experience Web3 technologies. Web3 events can feature speakers, workshops, and demos that can help people to understand the potential of Web3 and how to use it. DEO is a project on event organization in Web3 that has the potential to benefit society by increasing access, fostering transparency and trust, empowering individuals, creating economic opportunities, building communities, respecting data privacy, and contributing to education and innovation. These benefits align with the principles of decentralization and empowerment that Web3 technologies aim to bring to the internet and the broader society.
